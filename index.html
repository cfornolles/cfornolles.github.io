Biography

Curt Fornolles is a diligent individual who moved to the United States in pursuit of the American Dream. With a strong aspiration to become a Cybersecurity Threat Hunter, he diligently develops his skills through a combination of College Education, Cybersecurity Certifications, and Networking with industry professionals. Having worked in the restaurant industry for 8 years, he made a successful transition into the Information Technology in 2021. He finds immense satisfaction in resolving Information Technology issues and ensuring security, which led him to choose the path of a Threat Hunter after shadowing a professional in the field. Recognizing the excitement of analyzing potential threats and vulnerabilities within IT infrastructures, he is driven to fortify and secure critical systems.
